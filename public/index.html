How to play

  • move the mouse with the wad and space keys
  • don't get eaten by the cats
  • blow up the president

It is 2101, the year of our lord Jesus Christ, who died for our sins. You are a undercover mouse trained by MOUSE MILITIA to kill 34th president Abraham Clinton in the opal office. He hath done'st a very bad thing by opening up a wormhole to the planet Sector 8, where aliens plan to legalise marajuana! Stop those evil aliens and their early colludites, and ride up to claim the land that was once ours one hundred million years ago. Peace to all of the friends who shelter here, and divest us from you dark truths Oh Patan.
